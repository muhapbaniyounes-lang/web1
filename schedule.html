Attendance Policy

  • Students must attend at least 75% of all lectures.
  • Students who miss more than 25% of classes will receive a warning.
  • Students who miss more than 50% of classes may be denied entry to the final exam.
  • Late arrivals (more than 10 minutes) are recorded as "Late".
  • Three "Late" records count as one absence.
  • Medical excuses must be submitted within one week of the absence.
