About This System

The Attendance Management System is a web-based application developed as a project for the Web 1 (WEB101) course at the University of Jordan. It allows instructors to track student attendance, manage student records, and generate attendance reports.


Features:

  • Mark daily attendance (Present, Absent, Late)
  • Add and manage student records
  • Search students by ID or name
  • View attendance reports and statistics
  • View class schedule and important dates

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How is attendance recorded?

A: The instructor marks each student as Present, Absent, or Late at the beginning of each lecture.


Q: What happens if I miss too many classes?

A: Students who miss more than 25% of classes receive a warning. Missing more than 50% may result in being denied entry to the final exam.


Q: Can I submit a medical excuse?

A: Yes, medical excuses must be submitted to the instructor within one week of the absence.


Q: How can I check my attendance record?

A: You can view your attendance record on the Reports page.
